Adana kebab has a bold, savory, and spicy flavor characterized
by minced lamb or beef infused with a blend of red pepper flakes,
cumin, and sumac. The meat has a smoky taste from being grilled
over charcoal or an open flame, and its texture is firm yet juicy.
The spices and the quality of the ground meat are essential to its
unique flavor profile.

A chicken doner kebab is a delicious combination of savory, spiced,
and juicy chicken, balanced by the cool, tangy notes of a garlic yogurt
sauce and the fresh, crisp texture of vegetables, all wrapped in a soft,
warm flatbread.

A lamb shish kebab is defined by the bold, earthy flavor of the lamb,
the smoky notes from the grill, and the tender texture achieved through
marination, all complemented by fresh, tangy sauces and vegetables.

A classic chicken over rice is a symphony of contrasting flavors and textures:
the warm, spiced chicken, the fragrant, buttery rice, the cool and creamy white
sauce, the fiery kick of the hot sauce, and the fresh crunch of the vegetables.
It's a satisfying, comforting, and well-balanced meal in a single bowl.
